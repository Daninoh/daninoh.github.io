Hello! I'm Daniel!


I am a researcher, designer, maker, and artist currently exploring learning, technology, and creativity in Cambridge, MA.

I am pursuing a Master of Education in Learning Design, Innovation, and Technology at the Harvard Graduate School of Education. I am also a Research Assistant at Project Zero's Designing Learning Spaces Lab under Dr. Daniel Wilson, where I am researching spatial affordances for learning. You can also find me working at the Education Innovation Studio in Gutman Library, where I help people with laser cutters, 3D printers, and all sorts of maker things!

I hold a Bachelor of Architecture with University & College Honors from Carnegie Mellon University, with minors in Human-Computer Interaction and Design for Learning.

I also draw... a lot! You can check out my illustrations here and traditional drawings here!
